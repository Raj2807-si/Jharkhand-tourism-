Sarhul Festival

Sarhul is the most popular festival among the tribes of Jharkhand. It is celebrated during the spring season when the Sal trees get new flowers.

Chhau Dance

Chhau is a semi-classical Indian dance with martial, tribal, and folk origins. The Seraikella Chhau takes its name from Seraikela in Jharkhand.
